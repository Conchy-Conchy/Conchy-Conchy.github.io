About me

Introduction Link to heading

Hi!

This is Xin Xiong, a neophyte of OS (security) research, currently studying in secsys lab at Fudan University. I’ve been doing some researchs about bug detection in OS kernels with static analysis and fuzzing since 2020. And I’ve submitted some modest patches to Linux kernel, most of which accepted by developers.

I’m quite intrigued by the magic of OS itself, which drives me crazy and yen to explore more in depth. I’m also enthralled by the kaleidoscopic real-world kernel exploitations, which I quite enjoy analyzing it despite not being able to do it myself :(

Recently, I’m working on QEMU translation optimizations, which I also find quite interesting of. The internals are so cool!

Welcome to contact me through email at itsconchy@gmail.com if we share the same interests! (but no spam please)
